After registering the app, copy the Client ID from the _Overview_ section and generate/copy the Client Secret from the _Certificates & secrets_ section of the App page. On Composio's [Integrations](https://app.composio.dev/apps) page, find One Drive and add a new integration. Paste your Client ID and Secret, then Save to connect One Drive.
