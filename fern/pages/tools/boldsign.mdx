## Overview

### Enum
`BOLDSIGN`

### Description
BoldSign is an easy-to-use and secure electronic signature solution.
    With BoldSign you can quickly complete your contracts, accept offers,
    sign NDAs, approve invoices, confirm service appointments, onboard new
    employees, and more with just few clicks.
