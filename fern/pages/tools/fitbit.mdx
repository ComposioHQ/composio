## Overview

### Enum
`FITBIT`

### Description
Fitbit is a line of wireless-enabled wearable technology, physical
    fitness monitors and activity trackers such as smartwatches,
    pedometers and monitors for heart rate, quality of sleep, and stairs
    climbed as well as related software.
