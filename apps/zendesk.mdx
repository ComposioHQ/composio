Find the _Redirect URLs_ field and configure the below Redirect URL:

```
https://backend.composio.dev/api/v1/auth-apps/zendesk/connection/oauth2/add
```

Your _Unique Identifier_ is the Client ID, and your _Secret token_ is the Client Secret. On Composio's [supported apps](https://app.composio.dev/apps) page, find Zendesk and click "Enable". Paste your Client ID and Secret, then Save to connect Zendesk.
